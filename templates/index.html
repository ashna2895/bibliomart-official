Bibliomart is an online platform to sell and buy used books. Our target customers are students and book lovers. In our system we do not give a deadline. The customers will be provided with clear indexed lists based on categories like fiction, non-fiction, textbooks, popularity, pricing, authors, etc. They can buy books from the store with a guarantee that it will be bought back from them if returned in good condition, whenever they chose to do so. Payment can be done through our secure online gateways. We aim to provide a hassle free experience for all our users.
